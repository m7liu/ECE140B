Project Idea:

Have you ever found yourself to be in a bind over your form during sports? Your basketball throw is always a bit off, swing the bat always off kilter, or even arrows flying off to Narnia. Well, you are in luck. Our product idea intends to fix exactly that. The Limb Movement Tracker (not the actual name) is a series of wristbands that can connect to your phone or computer and tell you where your limbs are during your sporting attempts. Better yet, within the preset sport(s), it can tell you what you are doing wrong. Too fast of a rise, too slow of a release, or even the curse of gravity. So now you know exactly what is going on, and you can see it on your screen to how to fix that problem.

note: some brainpower may be required to process visual data
