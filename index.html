Project Idea:

Limb Movement Tracker

Have you ever found yourself to be in a bind over your form during sports? Your basketball throw is always a bit off, swing the bat always off kilter, or even arrows flying off to Narnia. Well, you are in luck. Our product idea intends to fix exactly that. The Limb Movement Tracker (not the actual name) is a series of wristbands that can connect to your phone or computer and tell you where your limbs are during your sporting attempts. Better yet, within the preset sport(s), it can tell you what you are doing wrong. Too fast of a rise, too slow of a release, or even the curse of gravity. So now you know exactly what is going on, and you can see it on your screen to how to fix that problem.

note:some brainpower may be required to process visual data

Our Product: (Name is still pending XD)

Our product is the simple and cheap solution to capture the motion of your limbs. If you have ever wanted to record yourself playing basketball, archery, and any sport, you know it's an impossible task without someone or something holding a camera. So there are very few choices to correct your form except to relive the experience and doing it again, and again, and again. Our product is here to fix this. If you can't hold the camera yourself then let some other device record you. We use a simple combination of lightweight hardware strapped onto key areas of your limbs to record your movement. So when you draw back the string, coil up for a jump, or pass the ball, you will know exactly what you did and how you did it via our product. It will record your movement with the lightweight devices, display it on a screen, so you can watch improvement over time and fix mistakes with tangible feedback.

What we want to bring to you:

Our motion tracker (FormView) helps new and amateur athletes who want to track their progress to achieve the perfect form by snapshotting mistakes during sports and gives the opportunity for self-improvement when practicing alone, unlike Qualisys and Xsens.
